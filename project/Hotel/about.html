About Hotel PSM

PSM Hotel is a 20-minute drive from Airport. Our luxurious limousine service offers smooth passage from the airport to our lakeshore pavilion where you will be met by our gracious Raffles Butlers, ready to escort you to your private boat transfer tailed by migratory birds.
